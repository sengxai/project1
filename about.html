Hello!

My name is Gaohli and also go by the name of Gaoneng. I am the owner of Vintage Ideal Cards!

I started doing this card business when I was in my senior year of highschool. How I started getting into making cards was when I first entered in a custom Christmas card contest at the Boys & Girls Club in Wausau. I entered in not knowing that my card would be chosen for this contest. The reason why I do this now is because I love art, I love drawing, and learning creative ideas. As I started drawing I've decided why not give the cards a try?

There is another part of why I am doing this is because, I wanted to incorporate my own culture into my own cards. I wanted to express the messages behind the Hmong symbols and drawings. With a traditional and modern day mix it I would feel more of an attachment to the products I make.
